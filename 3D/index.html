3D Section

In this section you will find some examples of my work.

My expertise lies in the intersection of design, technology, and engineering, creating functional and sustainable designs that balance aesthetic appeal with technical precision.

I welcome challenges and am constantly seeking opportunities to stretch my skills, learn new techniques, and bring client visions to life in the digital realm.

Technique

3D Modeling and Digital Simulation - I realize designed models are not only aesthetically pleasing, but are also perfect for digital simulations, helping to predict real-world behavior.

Computer Aided Design (CAD) - I use several CAD software to create 2D and 3D models. I am proficient in some software including AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Shapr3D and Blender. I am also learning Unreal Engine 5.

Computer Aided Engineering (CAE) - I perform complex finite element analysis (FEA), computational fluid dynamics (CFD), multibody dynamics (MBD), among others.
